<p><ul><li>Combine</li><li>Harvesting Robots</li><li>Forage Harvesters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision harvesting machines are divided into three main types: combines, harvesting robots, and forage harvesters. Combines are versatile machines that can harvest a variety of crops such as grains, corn, and soybeans. Harvesting robots are an emerging technology that uses artificial intelligence and robotics to autonomously harvest fruits and vegetables. Forage harvesters are specialized machines used to harvest high-moisture crops such as grass, corn, and sorghum for animal feed. Each type of precision harvesting machine offers unique benefits for farmers looking to improve efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
