<p><p>Automatic Number Plate Recognition Systems can be classified into two primary market types - Stationary and Portable. Stationary systems are fixed in one location, typically at entry and exit points of parking lots, toll booths, or surveillance checkpoints. Portable systems, on the other hand, can be easily moved and deployed in various locations for temporary monitoring or law enforcement purposes. Both types of systems use cameras and software to capture and read license plate information for security, surveillance, and access control applications.</p></p>
