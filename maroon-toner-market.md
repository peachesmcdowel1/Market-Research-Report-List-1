<p><ul><li>Mass Tone</li><li>Tint Tone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maroon toner is available in various market types such as Mass Tone and Tint Tone. Mass Tone refers to the pure, concentrated color of the toner without any added white or black. This type of toner is used for achieving intense and bold maroon shades. Tint Tone, on the other hand, consists of maroon toner mixed with white to create softer, lighter shades of maroon. This type is commonly used for creating pastel or diluted maroon tones.</p></p>
