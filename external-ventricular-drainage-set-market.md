<p><p>The External Ventricular Drainage Set market includes various types such as Valves, Shunts, External Drainage Systems, and Others. Valves are used to regulate the flow of cerebrospinal fluid in the brain. Shunts are tube-like devices that help divert excess fluid away from the brain. External Drainage Systems are used to drain fluid from the brain externally. Other types of devices may include catheters or monitoring systems. Each type serves a specific function in managing intracranial pressure and maintaining proper cerebral fluid flow.</p></p>
