<p><p>Wear bushings are components used in various industries to reduce friction and wear between two mechanical parts. There are two main types of wear bushings: metals and polymers. Metal wear bushings are typically made of bronze, steel, or stainless steel, offering high strength and durability. They are commonly used in heavy-duty applications such as automotive or industrial machinery. On the other hand, polymer wear bushings are made of materials like nylon or PTFE, providing low friction and high resistance to chemicals and moisture. They are often used in light to medium-duty applications, including electronics or furniture.</p></p>
