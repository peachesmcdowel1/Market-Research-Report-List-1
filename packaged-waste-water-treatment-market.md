<p><p>Packaged waste water treatment market consists of different types such as MBR (Membrane Bioreactor), MBBR (Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor), SBR (Sequential Batch Reactor), and Extended Aeration. MBR systems use membrane technology to separate solids and liquids, MBBR uses biofilm carriers for treatment, SBR operates in batches for nutrient removal, and Extended Aeration provides long aeration times for microbial activity. Each of these market types offer unique advantages and are used in various applications for efficient waste water treatment.</p></p>
