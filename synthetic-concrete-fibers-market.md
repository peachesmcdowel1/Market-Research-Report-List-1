<p><p>Synthetic concrete fibers are categorized into two main types: Macro-Synthetic Fibers and Micro-Synthetic Fibers. Macro-Synthetic Fibers are larger in size and are typically used to improve the cracking resistance and durability of concrete structures. On the other hand, Micro-Synthetic Fibers are smaller in size and are often used to enhance the toughness and impact resistance of concrete. Both types of synthetic fibers help to reinforce concrete and enhance its overall performance in various construction applications.</p></p>
