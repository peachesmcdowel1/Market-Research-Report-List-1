<p><p>Industrial Calcium Chloride is available in two main types: Granular Industrial Calcium Chloride and Powdery Industrial Calcium Chloride. Granular Industrial Calcium Chloride is a solid form of calcium chloride that is commonly used for dust control on unpaved roads, ice melting, and as a desiccant. Powdery Industrial Calcium Chloride, on the other hand, is a fine powder form of calcium chloride that is often used in concrete acceleration, drilling fluids, and industrial processes where a quick-dissolving form is required. Both types have various applications across industries.</p></p>
