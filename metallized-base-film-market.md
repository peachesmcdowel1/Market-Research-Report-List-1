<p><p>Metallized base films are commonly used in packaging and printing industries. The three main types of metallized base films are BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film, PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film, and CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film. BOPP film is known for its high tensile strength and excellent printability. PET film is valued for its transparency and barrier properties. CPP film is flexible and provides good heat sealing capabilities. Each type of metallized base film serves a specific purpose in various applications within the packaging and printing industries.</p></p>
