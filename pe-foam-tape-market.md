<p><p>PE Foam Tape is available in two main types - Double Sided and Single Sided. Double Sided PE Foam Tape has adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for applications where both surfaces need to be securely bonded. Single Sided PE Foam Tape has adhesive on only one side, providing cushioning and protection for surfaces. Both types are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics for bonding, mounting, and insulation applications. The choice between the two depends on specific project requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
