<p><p>Bio-imaging Market refers to the use of different imaging techniques for visualizing biological structures and processes in various medical fields. X-ray Radiography involves using X-rays to capture images of bone structures, whereas Medical Ultrasonography uses high-frequency sound waves to generate images of internal organs. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) utilizes strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of body parts. Elastography measures tissue stiffness to detect abnormalities. Other types of bio-imaging market include positron emission tomography (PET), computed tomography (CT), and optical imaging techniques, all aimed at providing valuable diagnostic information for medical professionals.</p></p>
