<p><p>Specialty aluminas market consists of various types such as Aluminium Trihydroxide(ATH), Activated Aluminas, Boehmite, Calcined Alumina, Tabular Alumina, and Fused Alumina. Calcined alumina is commonly used in refractories and ceramics. Tabular alumina is known for its high mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance. Fused alumina is used in abrasive applications due to its hardness.</p></p>
