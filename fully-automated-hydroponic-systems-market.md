<p><ul><li>Open System</li><li>Closed System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fully Automated Hydroponic Systems come in two main market types: Open System and Closed System. </p><p>Open System hydroponic systems continuously recirculate nutrient solution, allowing for better oxygenation of roots but requiring more maintenance. Closed System hydroponic systems utilize a reservoir to supply nutrients to the plants, with little to no nutrient loss. These systems are more efficient and require less maintenance but can be more expensive initially. Both systems offer advantages and disadvantages depending on the needs and preferences of the grower.</p></p>
