<p><p>Plasma derived medicine refers to medical products that are derived from human plasma, the fluid portion of blood. There are different types of plasma derived medicine available in the market. </p><p>Albumin is a protein found in plasma and is used to maintain blood volume in patients with low protein levels. </p><p>Coagulation factors are proteins that help in blood clotting and are used to treat bleeding disorders like hemophilia. </p><p>Immunoglobulins are antibodies that help in fighting infections and are used in the treatment of immune deficiencies and autoimmune diseases. </p><p>Other plasma derived medicines include factors like alpha-1 antitrypsin, which is used in the treatment of genetic disorders.</p></p>
