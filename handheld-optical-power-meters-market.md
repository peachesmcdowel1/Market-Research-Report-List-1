<p><p>The Handheld Optical Power Meters market is categorized into different types based on the number of channels they support. Single Channel power meters are equipped with only one channel input, allowing measurement and analysis of power in one optical signal path. 2 Channel power meters have two channel inputs, enabling simultaneous monitoring of power in two different paths. Similarly, 4 Channel power meters support four channel inputs, providing measurements in multiple optical signal paths. The "Other" market segment refers to any power meters that do not fit into the aforementioned categories, such as those with more than four channels or specialized features.</p></p>
