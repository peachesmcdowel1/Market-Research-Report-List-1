<p><p>The Mobile Payment SD Card market can be categorized into two main types: Independence and Inlay. Independence refers to SD cards that act as standalone payment devices, allowing users to make transactions without the need for a separate device. Inlay refers to SD cards that are embedded into other devices, such as smartphones or tablets, to enable mobile payments. Both types offer convenient and secure payment options for consumers, with Independence providing flexibility and Inlay offering seamless integration with existing technology.</p></p>
