<p><p>Flooring foams are materials used in flooring applications to provide cushioning, insulation, and sound absorption. The three main types of flooring foams are Polyethylene, Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), and Polyurethane. Polyethylene foam is lightweight and durable, making it ideal for underlayment in laminate flooring. EVA foam is soft and flexible, commonly used in yoga mats and children's play mats. Polyurethane foam is known for its resilience and shock-absorption properties, making it suitable for sports flooring and commercial spaces.</p></p>
