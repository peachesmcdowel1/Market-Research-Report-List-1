<p><ul><li>Cemented Femoral Hip Stems</li><li>Cementless Femoral Hip Stems</li><li>Hybrid Femoral Hip Stems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The femoral hip stem market is categorized into three main types: cemented femoral hip stems, cementless femoral hip stems, and hybrid femoral hip stems. Cemented femoral hip stems utilize bone cement to secure the implant in place, while cementless femoral hip stems rely on bone ingrowth for stability. Hybrid femoral hip stems combine elements of both cemented and cementless designs, providing a balance of immediate fixation and long-term stability. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations for surgeons and patients.</p></p>
