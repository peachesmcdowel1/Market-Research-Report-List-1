<p><p>Deep fryer cleaning chemicals can be classified into three main types: powder chemicals, foam chemicals, and tablets chemicals. Powder chemicals are in a powdered form and are dissolved in water before being used to clean the fryer. Foam chemicals create a foaming action that helps to penetrate and lift grease and grime in the fryer. Tablets chemicals are pre-measured tablets that dissolve in water to create a cleaning solution. Each type of chemical offers a convenient and effective solution for deep fryer maintenance.</p></p>
