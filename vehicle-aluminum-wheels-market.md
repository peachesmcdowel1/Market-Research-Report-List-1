<p><p>The Vehicle Aluminum Wheels Market can be divided into three main types based on the manufacturing process: Casting Process, Forging Process, and Semi-Solid Die Forging Process. The Casting Process involves pouring molten aluminum into a mold to form the wheel. Forging Process uses high pressure to shape the aluminum wheel into its desired form. Semi-Solid Die Forging Process combines elements of casting and forging, resulting in a wheel with improved strength and lighter weight. Each process offers unique benefits for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
