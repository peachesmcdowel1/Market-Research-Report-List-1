<p><ul><li>Oxides Ceramics</li><li>Non-Oxides Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Porous ceramics market can be divided into two main types: oxides ceramics and non-oxides ceramics. Oxides ceramics are materials that contain oxygen as the major component, such as alumina and zirconia. Non-oxides ceramics, on the other hand, do not contain oxygen as the major component and include materials like silicon carbide and silicon nitride. Both types of porous ceramics are widely used in various applications due to their unique properties, including high temperature resistance, hardness, and chemical inertness.</p></p>
