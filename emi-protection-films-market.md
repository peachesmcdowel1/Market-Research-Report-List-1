<p><ul><li>Conductive Adhesive Type</li><li>Metal Alloy Type</li><li>Metal Microneedle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EMI protection films come in various types to shield electronic devices from electromagnetic interference. Conductive adhesive type films use conductive materials in the adhesive layer to dissipate electromagnetic energy. Metal alloy type films are made of metal alloys such as nickel or copper to block electromagnetic waves. Metal microneedle type films have tiny metallic needles that absorb and reflect electromagnetic energy. Each type offers unique benefits in protecting devices and ensuring their proper functioning in environments with high electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
