<p><p>Transparent photovoltaic (PV) glass for buildings is available in two main market types: fully transparent and semi-transparent. Fully transparent PV glass allows for maximum visible light transmission while generating electricity, making it ideal for applications where aesthetics are a priority. Semi-transparent PV glass, on the other hand, balances the need for visible light transmission and electricity generation, often used in buildings where some level of opacity is acceptable. Both types of PV glass offer a sustainable and innovative solution for integrating solar power generation into building designs.</p></p>
