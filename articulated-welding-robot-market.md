<p><p>Articulated welding robots are categorized into different types based on their specific welding functionalities. Spot welding robots are designed for applications that require a series of spot welds in a specific pattern. These robots are commonly used in automotive manufacturing. On the other hand, arc welding robots are used for applications that involve continuous welding along a seam or joint. These robots are versatile and can handle various materials and thicknesses. Both types of welding robots offer precision, efficiency, and consistency in welding operations.</p></p>
