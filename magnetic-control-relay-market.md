<p><p>Magnetic control relays are categorized into two main market types, single-phase, and three-phase. Single-phase relays are designed to control electrical circuits with a single phase of power supply, commonly found in residential and small commercial applications. On the other hand, three-phase relays are used in larger industrial settings where three-phase power is required to control heavy machinery and equipment. Both types of relays provide reliable and efficient control over electrical circuits, catering to different power supply requirements in various applications.</p></p>
