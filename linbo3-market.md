<p><p>LiNbO3 is commonly used in two main markets: acoustic grade and optical grade. On the other hand, optical grade LiNbO3 is used in the production of devices such as modulators, switches, and frequency doublers for optical communication systems. Both markets require high-quality LiNbO3 crystals, but the specific properties and production processes may vary depending on the intended application.</p></p>
