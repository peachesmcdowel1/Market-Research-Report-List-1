<p><p>Pest control products market consists of three main types: chemical control, mechanical control, and biological control. Chemical control involves the use of pesticides or insecticides to eliminate pests. Mechanical control utilizes physical methods such as traps, barriers, or repellents to manage pests. Biological control employs the use of natural enemies like predators, parasites, or pathogens to control pest populations. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice depends on factors such as environmental impacts, efficacy, and specific pest management needs.</p></p>
