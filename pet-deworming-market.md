<p><strong>The Pet Deworming Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Deworming</li><li>External Deworming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pet deworming refers to the process of eliminating internal parasites (internal deworming) and external parasites (external deworming) from pets. Internal deworming involves the administration of medications, such as deworming tablets or injections, to eradicate worms residing in the digestive system. External deworming involves the use of shampoos, sprays, and topical solutions to eliminate parasites like fleas, ticks, or mites on the pet's fur or skin. Both types of deworming are crucial for maintaining the health and wellbeing of pets, preventing the spread of diseases, and ensuring a safe and comfortable living environment.</p></p>
