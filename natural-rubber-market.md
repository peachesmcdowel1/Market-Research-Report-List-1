<p><p>Natural rubber is categorized into different market types based on their processing methods and properties. Ribbed Smoked Sheet (RSS) is a traditional form of rubber produced through air-drying ribbed sheets. Technically Specified Rubber (TSR) is a higher-grade rubber produced through controlled processing. Latex is a liquid form of rubber tapped from trees. Other market types include compounded rubber products, block rubber, and centrifuged latex. Each market type has unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
