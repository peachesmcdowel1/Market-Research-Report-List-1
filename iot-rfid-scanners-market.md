<p><p>Fixed RFID scanners are stationary devices that are permanently installed in a specific location to automatically scan and track items as they pass by. These scanners are commonly used in warehouses, retail stores, and manufacturing facilities. Handheld RFID scanners, on the other hand, are portable devices that can be carried around by users to scan items on the go. They are typically used in industries such as logistics, healthcare, and transportation for inventory management and asset tracking purposes.</p></p>
