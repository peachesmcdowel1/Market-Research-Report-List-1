<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Aluminum Alloy Wheel market can be segmented into three main types based on production methods: Casting, Forging, and Other. Casting involves pouring molten aluminum into a mold to form the wheel shape, which is a popular and cost-effective method. Forging utilizes a process of shaping the aluminum by compressing it with high pressure, resulting in a stronger and lighter wheel. The Other category includes alternative methods such as spinning or machining aluminum to create the wheels. Each method has its own unique benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
