<p><p>Paraffin inhibitors are chemicals used to prevent the buildup of paraffin deposits in oil wells and pipelines, which can cause flow assurance issues. They work by dispersing the paraffin molecules to keep them from sticking together and forming blockages. Asphaltene inhibitors serve a similar function but target asphaltene deposits, which can also lead to flow restrictions. Both types of inhibitors are crucial in maintaining the efficiency and productivity of oil and gas production operations.</p></p>
