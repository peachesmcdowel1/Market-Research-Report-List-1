<p><p>The MOSFET Discretes market consists of three types: LV MOSFET, MV MOSFET, and HV MOSFET. MV MOSFET refers to Medium Voltage MOSFET, used in applications where moderate to high voltage levels are required. HV MOSFET stands for High Voltage MOSFET, designed for applications that demand high voltage operations. These three market segments cater to different voltage requirements and address the diverse needs of various industries.</p></p>
