<p><p>Software as a Service (SaaS) in cloud retail refers to the provision of complete software applications on a subscription basis, allowing retailers to access and use these applications via the internet. Platform as a Service (PaaS) involves providing retailers with a platform that enables them to develop, run, and manage their retail applications without worrying about the underlying infrastructure. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) market provides virtualized computing resources to retailers, like servers and storage, allowing them to build and manage their own applications or systems on these resources.</p></p>
