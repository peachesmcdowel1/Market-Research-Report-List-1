<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The POM Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homo-polyoxymethylene</li><li>Co-polyoxymethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POM sheets are available in two main market types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene sheets are made from pure polyoxymethylene resin, offering high stiffness, strength, and chemical resistance. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene sheets are a blend of polyoxymethylene and other copolymer resins, providing improved impact resistance and toughness. Both market types cater to different applications and requirements, with homo-polyoxymethylene preferred for high-performance and precision applications, while co-polyoxymethylene is favored for applications requiring more flexibility and impact resistance.</p></p>
