<p><p>The Agriculture Carbamide market is divided into two main types: Granular and Prilled carbamide. Granular carbamide is a type of fertilizer that is formed into small, solid pellets, while prilled carbamide is made up of small round beads. Granular carbamide is commonly used for crops that require slow-release nitrogen, while prilled carbamide is often used for crops that need a quick nitrogen boost.
