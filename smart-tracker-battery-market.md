<p><p>Smart tracker batteries come in two main types: traditional button batteries and rechargeable lithium batteries. Traditional button batteries are small, disposable batteries commonly used in smart trackers. They are inexpensive but need to be replaced regularly. Rechargeable lithium batteries, on the other hand, can be recharged multiple times, making them more cost-effective and environmentally friendly in the long run. They are also known for providing longer-lasting power compared to traditional button batteries. Both types cater to different consumer needs in the smart tracker market.</p></p>
