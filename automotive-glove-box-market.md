<p><p>The automotive glove box market consists of two main types: fixed type and tipping bucket type. Fixed type glove boxes are stationary and typically have a hinged door for easy access to storage space inside the car. Tipping bucket type glove boxes, on the other hand, have a design where the box can be tilted or tipped forward for easier access to its contents. Both types serve the same purpose of providing storage space for various items within reach of the driver and passengers.</p></p>
