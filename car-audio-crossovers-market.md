<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Car Audio Crossovers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car audio crossovers come in two main types - active and passive. Active crossovers require external power sources and are installed between the audio source and the amplifiers, allowing for more precise tuning and customization of sound frequencies. They provide greater control over the audio signal and are preferred by audio enthusiasts. On the other hand, passive crossovers do not require external power and are installed between the amplifiers and the speakers. They separate the audio signal into different frequency bands, directing each band to the appropriate speaker, resulting in improved sound quality. However, passive crossovers offer limited customization options compared to active crossovers.</p></p>
