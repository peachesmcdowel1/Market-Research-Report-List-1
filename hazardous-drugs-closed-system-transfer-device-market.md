<p><p>Hazardous Drug Closed System Transfer Device Market is segmented into three types - Closed Vial Access Devices, Closed Syringe Safety Devices, and Closed Bag/Line Access Devices. Closed Vial Access Devices refer to systems used to access and withdraw medication from vials, maintaining a closed environment. Closed Syringe Safety Devices are designed to prevent accidental needlestick injuries during the administration or disposal of hazardous drugs. Closed Bag/Line Access Devices are used for safe transfer of hazardous drugs between bags or lines, ensuring containment and preventing contamination. These devices play a crucial role in minimizing the risks associated with handling hazardous drugs.</p></p>
