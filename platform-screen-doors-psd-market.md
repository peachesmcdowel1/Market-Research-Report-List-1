<p><p>Platform Screen Doors (PSD) are safety systems installed at train stations to prevent accidents and ensure passenger safety. There are three main types of PSD markets: Full-Closed Type, which completely seals off the platform from the tracks; Semi-Closed Type, which have sliding doors that open when the train arrives; and Half-Height Type, which only cover half of the platform height. Each type offers varying levels of protection while allowing for efficient boarding and disembarking of passengers.</p></p>
