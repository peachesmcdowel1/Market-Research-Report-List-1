<p><p>Rotating electrical connectors are devices that allow electrical currents to pass between stationary and rotating parts of a system. Mercury-wetted rotating electrical connectors use a pool of mercury to facilitate the connection. Pancake rotating electrical connectors have a flat design for space-saving installations. Wireless rotating electrical connectors utilize electromagnetic induction or radio frequency technology to transmit power without physical contact. Each type caters to specific application needs and offers unique advantages in terms of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
