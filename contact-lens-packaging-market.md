<p><p>Contact lens packaging market is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable contact lenses are designed for one-time use and are discarded after a specified period, typically daily, bi-weekly, or monthly. They are convenient and hygienic, suitable for those who do not want to deal with cleaning routines. On the other hand, reusable contact lenses can be worn for an extended period, usually up to a year, with daily cleaning and maintenance. They are cost-effective and environmentally friendly options.</p></p>
