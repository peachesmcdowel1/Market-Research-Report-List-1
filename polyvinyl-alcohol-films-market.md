<p><p>Polyvinyl alcohol films come in various types catering to different needs. Quick dissolving films are designed to rapidly dissolve when in contact with water, making them suitable for products like laundry detergents. Dissolved films break down slowly, releasing their contents gradually, ideal for controlled release of medications. Undissolved films remain intact even when wet, useful for applications like packaging. Special films offer unique features such as increased flexibility or enhanced strength, making them versatile for various industries.</p></p>
