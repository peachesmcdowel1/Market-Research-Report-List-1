<p><p>Hotel reservation software is available in two main types: cloud-based and premise-based markets. In the cloud-based market, the software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. This allows hotels to have easy access to the software from multiple devices and locations, with the added benefit of scalability and regular updates. On the other hand, the premise-based market involves installing the software on the hotel's own servers. This gives hotels more control and customization options but requires higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
