<p><strong>The Thermal Insulating Mortars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inorganic Insulating Mortar</li><li>Organic Insulating Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal insulating mortars are categorized into two main types: inorganic and organic. Inorganic insulating mortars are made of materials like perlite, vermiculite, and mineral fibers, offering excellent fire resistance and durability. On the other hand, organic insulating mortars are composed of materials such as polystyrene beads or foam, providing good thermal insulation properties. Both types of mortars are used in construction to enhance energy efficiency and reduce heat loss in buildings, but they differ in their composition and specific performance characteristics.</p></p>
