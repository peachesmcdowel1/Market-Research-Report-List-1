<p><p>Green polyols are a type of eco-friendly alternative to conventional petroleum-based polyols used in various industries such as construction, automotive, and furniture. There are two main types of green polyols: polyether polyols and polyester polyols. Polyether polyols are made from organic compounds like propylene oxide and ethylene oxide, while polyester polyols are derived from renewable resources such as soybeans or corn. Both types of green polyols have gained popularity due to their reduced environmental impact and sustainability benefits.</p></p>
