<p><p>Electric water pumps (EWP) come in two main types: 12V EWP and 24V EWP. The 12V EWP is designed to operate at a lower voltage, making it suitable for smaller applications such as cars, motorcycles, and small water systems. On the other hand, the 24V EWP is built for larger systems that require more power, such as trucks, RVs, and industrial machinery. Both types of EWPs offer efficient water circulation and cooling capabilities, but the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
