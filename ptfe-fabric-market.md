<p><strong>The PTFE Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE Glass Fabric</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE Glass Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE fabric comes in two main types: porous and non-porous PTFE glass fabric. Porous PTFE glass fabric is designed to allow for air and liquid to pass through, making it ideal for applications like filtration and breathable membranes. Non-porous PTFE glass fabric, on the other hand, has a smooth surface that prevents liquids and gases from passing through, making it suitable for applications where a high level of impermeability is required, such as in chemical processing or protective clothing.</p></p>
