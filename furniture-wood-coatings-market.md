<p><p>Furniture wood coatings market offers various types including solvent-borne, waterborne, radiation-cured, powder, and other coatings. Solvent-borne coatings are known for their durability and resistance to scratches, while waterborne coatings are eco-friendly and easy to clean. Radiation-cured coatings are popular for their fast curing time and excellent hardness. Powder coatings provide a durable and even finish. Other coatings include acrylic, polyurethane, and polyester-based options that cater to specific furniture finishing needs. Each type offers unique benefits for different furniture applications.</p></p>
