<p><strong>The Low Power IoT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NB-IoT</li><li>LoRa</li><li>LTE-M</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-power IoT market encompasses three main types: NB-IoT, LoRa, and LTE-M. NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT) is a cellular technology providing long-range communication, low data rates, and low power consumption, making it suitable for applications such as smart metering and asset tracking. LoRa (Long Range) is a wireless technology that enables long-range communication with low power consumption, targeting use cases like smart cities and agriculture. LTE-M (Long-Term Evolution for Machines) operates on existing cellular networks, providing higher data rates, lower latency, and moderate power consumption, typically used for applications in healthcare and industrial sectors.</p></p>
