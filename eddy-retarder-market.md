<p><p>Eddy retarders can be classified into different market types based on their installation location. The first type is mounted on the transmission, where the retarder is integrated into the transmission system. The second type is installed on the rear axle, providing braking power directly to the axle. The third type is installed between the drive shafts, allowing for more precise control of the braking force. Other markets for eddy retarders may include custom installations in unique vehicles or specialized industrial applications.
