<p><p>The minimally invasive dermatabrasion machine market consists of two main types: crystal type and diamond type. On the other hand, diamond type machines use diamond-tipped wands to gently abrade the skin, making it a more precise and comfortable option. Although diamond type machines are generally more expensive, they offer better control and can be used on different skin types.</p></p>
