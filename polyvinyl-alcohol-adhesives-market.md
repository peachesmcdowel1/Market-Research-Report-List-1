<p><p>Polyvinyl alcohol adhesives are available in two main types based on viscosity - low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity adhesives are fluid and easy to spread, making them suitable for applications where fast and efficient bonding is required. On the other hand, high viscosity adhesives are thicker and offer better gap-filling capabilities, making them ideal for applications where a stronger bond is needed. Both types provide excellent adhesion properties and are widely used in various industries for bonding different materials.</p></p>
