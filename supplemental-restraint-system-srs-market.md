<p><p>Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) in vehicles includes various types of safety features such as driver and passenger front airbags, front seat-mounted airbags, curtain side-impact airbags, and pretensioners on the seatbelts. Driver and passenger front airbags provide protection in the event of a frontal collision, while front seat-mounted airbags offer additional protection for the occupants. Curtain side-impact airbags deploy from the ceiling to protect passengers in side collisions, and pretensioners on seatbelts remove slack in the belts during a crash to reduce forward movement of the occupants.</p></p>
