<p><p>A Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) Device is a small implantable device that delivers electrical stimulation directly to the spinal cord to help manage chronic pain. The device consists of a small pulse generator that is connected to thin wires, called leads, which are placed in the epidural space near the spinal cord. The device works by sending electrical impulses that interfere with the pain signals traveling to the brain, thereby reducing the sensation of pain.</p><p>The Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic pain conditions, advancements in technology leading to more effective devices, and rising awareness about non-pharmacological pain management alternatives.
