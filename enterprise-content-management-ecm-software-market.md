<p><p>Enterprise Content Management (ECM) Software market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based ECM software is a solution hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet, providing users with flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises ECM software is installed and maintained on local servers within an organization's infrastructure, giving more control and security but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. These two market types offer businesses different options to manage, organize, and retrieve their enterprise content efficiently based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
