<p><p>Aramid fibers are categorized into two main types: Para-aramid fibers and Meta-aramid fibers. Para-aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, are known for their high strength and heat resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and protection. On the other hand, Meta-aramid fibers, like Nomex, offer flame resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for protective clothing and industrial applications. Both types of aramid fibers have unique properties that cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
