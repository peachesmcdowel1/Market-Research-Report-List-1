<p><p>Single strip seal expansion joints are used in bridges and highways to accommodate movement caused by temperature changes and vehicle loads. They come in two main types: strip seal expansion joints with straight head and strip seal expansion joints with bending head. The straight head type is used when movements are limited to one direction, while the bending head type allows for movement in multiple directions. Both types provide a watertight seal to protect the structure from water damage and debris.</p></p>
