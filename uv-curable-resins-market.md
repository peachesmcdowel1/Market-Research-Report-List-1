<p><p>UV Curable Resins are widely categorized into two main types: Radical UV-curable Resin and Cationic UV-curable Resin. Radical UV-curable Resin polymerizes when exposed to UV light, forming strong and durable bonds. On the other hand, Cationic UV-curable Resin initiates polymerization through the use of cationic photoinitiators under UV light. Both types of resins are used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction for applications like coatings, adhesives, and 3D printing due to their fast curing and environmentally friendly properties.</p></p>
