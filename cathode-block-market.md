<p><strong>The Cathode Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-graphitic Type</li><li>Graphitic Type</li><li>Graphitized Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cathode blocks are used in the production of aluminum. There are three main types of cathode blocks based on their carbon content: Semi-graphitic type, which has a lower carbon content and is suitable for smaller aluminum smelters; Graphitic type, which has a moderate carbon content and is used in medium-sized smelters; and Graphitized type, which has a high carbon content and is ideal for larger smelters. Each type offers different levels of thermal conductivity, resistance to oxidation, and durability, catering to the specific needs of different smelting operations.</p></p>
