<p><p>Diabetes drug therapy market comprises three main types of administration methods: oral, subcutaneous, and intravenous. Oral medications are taken by mouth and are convenient for patients as they can be self-administered. Subcutaneous injections involve injecting the medication just beneath the skin using a syringe or an insulin pen. Intravenous administration involves delivering the drug directly into the bloodstream using an IV line or infusion pump. Each method has its own advantages and considerations, and the choice depends on the patient's specific needs and the type of medication prescribed.</p></p>
