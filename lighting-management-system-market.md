One of the major trends is the increasing adoption of Internet of Things (IoT) technology in lighting management systems. IoT integration allows lighting systems to be interconnected and controlled remotely through centralized platforms, providing enhanced functionality and convenience.</p><p>Another trend is the growing popularity of wireless lighting controls.
