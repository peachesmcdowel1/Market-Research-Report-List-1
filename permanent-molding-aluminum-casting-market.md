<p><strong>The Permanent Molding Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>Slush</li><li>Low-pressure</li><li>Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent Molding Aluminum Casting refers to a casting process where reusable molds are used to create aluminum parts. Gravity casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold which allows gravity to fill the cavity. Slush casting involves rotating the mold to ensure even coverage. Low-pressure casting uses low pressure to fill the mold with metal. Vacuum casting removes air from the mold to improve metal flow. Each type of casting method offers unique advantages in terms of quality, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
