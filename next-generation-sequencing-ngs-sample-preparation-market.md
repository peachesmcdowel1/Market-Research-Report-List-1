<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) Sample Preparation Market is categorized into different types based on the sequencing techniques used. These include Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS), Ion Semiconductor Sequencing, Single Molecule Real Time (SMRT) Sequencing, and Nanopore Sequencing. SBS involves the detection of base pairs as they are incorporated into the growing DNA strand. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing utilizes the detection of hydrogen ions. SMRT Sequencing involves the real-time monitoring of DNA synthesis using fluorescent labeling. Nanopore Sequencing uses nanopores to detect changes in current as DNA strands pass through them. Each technique has its unique advantages and applications in DNA analysis and sequencing.</p></p>
