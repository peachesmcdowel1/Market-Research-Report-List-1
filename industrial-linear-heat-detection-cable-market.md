<p><p>Industrial Linear Heat Detection Cable is a type of sensor cable used for detecting heat in industrial environments. It consists of a polymer outer sheath that houses a twisted pair of insulated conductors. When heat is detected, the insulation melts and the conductors make contact, triggering an alarm signal. This type of cable is commonly used in applications where early detection of heat is critical, such as in industrial plants, warehouses, and tunnels.</p><p>The Industrial Linear Heat Detection Cable Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period.
