Cathode materials are used to store and release energy in the battery, while anode materials facilitate the movement of ions. The lithium-ion battery separator prevents short circuits within the battery, and the electrolyte conducts ions between the cathode and anode. These materials are crucial components in the production of batteries for various electronic devices and electric vehicles.</p></p>
