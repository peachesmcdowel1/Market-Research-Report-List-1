<p><p>Commercial electric forklift batteries come in two main types: lead-acid and Li-Ion. Lead-acid batteries are the traditional choice for forklifts, offering a lower upfront cost but requiring regular maintenance and shorter lifespan. On the other hand, Li-Ion batteries are becoming increasingly popular due to their longer lifespan, faster charging times, and maintenance-free operation. While Li-Ion batteries have a higher initial cost, they provide cost savings in the long run through improved efficiency and reduced downtime.</p></p>
