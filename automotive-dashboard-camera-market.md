<p><p>The automotive dashboard camera market is divided into two main types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel dashboard cameras record video footage from one perspective, usually facing the road ahead. Multi-channel dashboard cameras, on the other hand, have multiple cameras that can record video from different angles simultaneously. Single channel cameras are typically more affordable and simpler to use, while multi-channel cameras offer more comprehensive coverage and a higher level of security for the vehicle.</p></p>
