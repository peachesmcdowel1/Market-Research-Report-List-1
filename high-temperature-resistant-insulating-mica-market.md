<p><p>High Temperature Resistant Insulating Mica Market consists of two main types: Mica Plate and Mica Paper. On the other hand, Mica Paper is a flexible sheet made from thin mica flakes, providing good thermal and electrical insulation. It is often utilized in high temperature industrial applications where flexibility is required. Both types cater to the growing demand for reliable and durable insulation materials in various industries.</p></p>
