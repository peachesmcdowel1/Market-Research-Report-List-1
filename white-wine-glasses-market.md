<p><p>White wine glasses are available in two main market types: stemmed and stemless. Stemmed glasses have a long, thin stem that allows the drinker to hold the glass without affecting the temperature of the wine. This design also prevents fingerprints on the bowl of the glass. On the other hand, stemless glasses have a shorter, more modern design without a stem. They are often more stable and less likely to tip over. Both types of white wine glasses are popular choices among consumers, each offering unique benefits for enjoying wine.</p></p>
