Analog tilt sensors provide a continuous output signal proportional to the tilt angle, while digital tilt sensors provide a discrete output signal indicating a specific angle threshold. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and stability of aerospace vehicles and equipment.</p></p>
