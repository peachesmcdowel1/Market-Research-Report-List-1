<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Conductive Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Al Paste</li><li>Ag Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photovoltaic conductive paste is used in the manufacturing of solar cells to create electrical connections. The two main types in the market are Al paste and Ag paste. Al paste, made with aluminum particles, is more cost-effective and widely used for its excellent conductivity. Ag paste, containing silver particles, is known for its superior conductivity but comes at a higher cost. Both pastes play a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of solar cells, with manufacturers choosing between them based on their specific needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
