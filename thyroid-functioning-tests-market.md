<p><p>The Thyroid Functioning Tests market comprises different types of tests that help evaluate the functioning of the thyroid gland. TSH (Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone) Test is used to measure the levels of TSH in the blood and assess thyroid gland activity. FT3 (Free Triiodothyronine) Test measures the levels of free T3 hormone, which is responsible for regulating metabolism. FT4 (Free Thyroxine) Test, on the other hand, measures the levels of free T4 hormone, which controls various body functions. These tests aid in diagnosing and managing thyroid disorders, ensuring optimal thyroid function.</p></p>
