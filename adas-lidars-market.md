<p><p>ADAS Lidars, also known as Advanced Driver Assistance Systems Lidars, are sensors used in vehicles to provide detailed and accurate 3D mapping of the surrounding environment. They utilize laser light pulses to measure distances and create high-resolution images for applications such as collision avoidance, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking.</p><p>The ADAS Lidars market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period. The growth of this market is driven by increasing demand for safer and more efficient driving experiences, stringent government regulations for vehicle safety, and technological advancements in autonomous driving systems. The integration of Lidars in next-generation vehicles is expected to further boost market growth.</p><p>One of the key trends in the ADAS Lidars market is the development of solid-state Lidars, which offer improved reliability and performance compared to traditional mechanical Lidars. These solid-state sensors are smaller, more cost-effective, and have a longer lifespan, making them ideal for mass production in the automotive industry. Additionally, advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms are enhancing the capabilities of ADAS Lidars, enabling more sophisticated and accurate detection of objects and obstacles on the road.</p></p>
