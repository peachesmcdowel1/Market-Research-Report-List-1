<p><p>Plastic pipe jointing and welding involves different methods for joining or bonding plastic pipes together. Mechanical fastening refers to using various fittings, connectors, or clamps to hold the pipes together. Adhesive bonding involves using specific adhesives to create a strong bond between the pipes. Welding, on the other hand, involves melting the plastic material at the joint and joining the pipes by fusion. These different methods cater to various requirements and preferences in the market for plastic pipe jointing and welding.</p></p>
