<p><ul><li>On-premises</li><li>Cloud Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Restaurant inventory management software is available in two market types: on-premises and cloud-based. </p><p>On-premises software is installed directly on the restaurant's premises and runs on the restaurant's own servers, requiring IT infrastructure and maintenance. It offers high security and customization options, but it can be more expensive upfront and may require IT expertise.</p><p>Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as there is no need for upfront investment in hardware or infrastructure. Updates and support are usually managed by the vendor, allowing for easy implementation and accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection.</p></p>
