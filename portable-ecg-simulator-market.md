They offer real-time data transmission and analysis, enabling healthcare professionals to monitor patients remotely. On the other hand, Offline ECG Simulators function without the need for internet connectivity. They are designed for on-site training purposes, allowing healthcare providers to simulate and practice ECG readings and interpretations without the need for a live patient or internet connection.</p></p>
