These factors increase the demand for effective medication management systems that can address the unique needs of elderly patients and patients with chronic conditions.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in healthcare technology, such as the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) algorithms in drug management systems, are also propelling market growth.
