<p><strong>The Inventory Tank Gauging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic ITG</li><li>Mechanical ITG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inventory tank gauging (ITG) market types can be classified into two categories: electronic ITG and mechanical ITG. </p><p>Electronic ITG relies on advanced technologies such as radar, ultrasonic, or guided wave to measure tank levels, temperatures, and other parameters. It offers accurate and real-time data, providing efficient monitoring and control of inventory levels.</p><p>On the other hand, mechanical ITG uses mechanical devices like floats, tapes, or servo gauges to measure tank levels. Although less advanced than electronic ITG, mechanical systems are reliable and cost-effective options for gauging inventory in tanks.</p><p>Both electronic and mechanical ITG technologies play crucial roles in monitoring and managing inventory levels, catering to different industry requirements and preferences.</p></p>
