Three common types of foam core materials are PET foam, PVC foam, and other foam cores. PET foam is known for its high strength-to-weight ratio, PVC foam is popular for its chemical resistance, and other foam cores encompass a variety of materials like polystyrene and polyurethane. These materials are used in various applications such as sandwich panels, insulation, and marine construction.</p></p>
