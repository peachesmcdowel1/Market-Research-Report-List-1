<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil Grease</li><li>Synthetic Oil Grease</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive grease is typically categorized into three main types: Mineral Oil Grease, Synthetic Oil Grease, and Others. Mineral oil grease is made from mineral oils and thickened with soap, providing good mechanical stability and high load-carrying capacity. Synthetic oil grease, on the other hand, is formulated with synthetic oils for enhanced performance in extreme conditions. The Others category may include specialty greases with unique formulations to meet specific requirements, such as high temperature or water resistance. Each type of grease offers distinct advantages for lubricating automotive components.</p></p>
