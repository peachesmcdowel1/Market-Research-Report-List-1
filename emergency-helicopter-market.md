<p><strong>The Emergency Helicopter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-rotor</li><li>Double-rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency helicopter market is divided into two main types based on their rotor systems: single-rotor and double-rotor helicopters. Single-rotor helicopters have one large main rotor and a tail rotor to provide stability and control. They are versatile and widely used for medical evacuations and search and rescue missions. Double-rotor helicopters, on the other hand, have two main rotors mounted one on top of the other. These helicopters have greater lifting capacity and load stability, making them suitable for large-scale emergency operations such as disaster relief efforts.</p></p>
