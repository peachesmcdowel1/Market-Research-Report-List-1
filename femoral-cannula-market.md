<p><strong>The Femoral Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The femoral cannula market consists of two types: unipolar and bipolar cannulas. Unipolar cannulas are used in cardiac surgeries to divert blood flow from the heart to a cardiopulmonary bypass machine for oxygenation. Bipolar cannulas, on the other hand, are utilized in surgeries to both divert and return blood to the heart without the need for a bypass machine. This allows for better oxygenation and reduces the risk of blood clot formation. Both types of cannulas play a crucial role in improving patient outcomes during cardiac procedures.</p></p>
