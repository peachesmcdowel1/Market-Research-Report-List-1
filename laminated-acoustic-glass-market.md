<p><p>Laminated Acoustic Glass market is categorized based on the types of interlayers used in the glass. The most common interlayer materials include Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB), Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), and Structural Glass Interlayer (SGP). PVB interlayers are known for their high sound reduction properties, while EVA interlayers offer improved clarity and UV resistance. SGP interlayers provide enhanced strength and durability. Other types of interlayers may be used for specific customization and performance requirements in the laminated acoustic glass market.</p></p>
