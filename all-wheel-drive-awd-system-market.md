<p><p>All-wheel Drive (AWD) System is available in two types - Manual and Automatic. In the Manual AWD system, the driver has control over when to engage the AWD mode, providing more control and flexibility. On the other hand, in the Automatic AWD system, the vehicle's computer automatically engages the AWD mode when it detects slipping or loss of traction, offering convenience and ease of use for the driver. Both types aim to improve traction and stability on various road conditions.</p></p>
