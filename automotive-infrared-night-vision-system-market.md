<p><strong>The Automotive Infrared Night Vision System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Infrared Night Vision System</li><li>Passive Infrared Night Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Infrared Night Vision System market consists of two main types: Active and Passive Infrared Night Vision Systems. Active systems use infrared light emitted from the vehicle to illuminate the road ahead, providing a clear image in complete darkness. Passive systems, on the other hand, detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, such as animals or pedestrians, to create a thermal image. Both systems enhance driver visibility and safety in low-light conditions, but they utilize different technologies to achieve this goal.</p></p>
