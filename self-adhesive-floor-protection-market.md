<p><p>Self-adhesive floor protection comes in two main types - felt type and foil type. Felt type protection is made of durable felt material that is easy to apply and provides scratch and impact resistance to floors. Foil type protection, on the other hand, is made of a thin layer of foil that is waterproof and ideal for protecting floors in high-traffic areas. Both types of self-adhesive floor protection are designed to prevent damage and stains to floors during construction, renovations, or moving processes.</p></p>
