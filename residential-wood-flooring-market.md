<p><p>Residential wood flooring market consists of two main types: Single-layer wood flooring and Multi-layer wood flooring. Single-layer wood flooring is made from a single piece of solid wood and is known for its classic look and durability. On the other hand, multi-layer wood flooring, also known as engineered wood flooring, is made up of multiple layers of wood pressed together. It is more resistant to moisture and humidity, making it ideal for areas such as basements and kitchens. Both types offer a variety of styles and finishes to suit different preferences and needs.</p></p>
