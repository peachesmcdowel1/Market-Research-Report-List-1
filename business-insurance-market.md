<p><p>Business insurance refers to a type of insurance policy that provides coverage for businesses against potential financial losses. Business insurance typically includes a variety of coverage options such as general liability insurance, property insurance, workers' compensation insurance, professional liability insurance, and business interruption insurance.</p><p>The market for business insurance has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Factors such as increasing awareness about the importance of insurance among businesses, growing compliance requirements, and the rise in business risks have contributed to the market's growth. With the growing threat of cyber attacks and data breaches, businesses are becoming more aware of the potential financial losses resulting from such incidents.
