<p><p>Passenger car security devices consist of immobilizers, vehicle alarms, central locking systems, and Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) systems. Immobilizers prevent unauthorized access to the vehicle by disabling the engine. Vehicle alarms alert the owner and surrounding individuals in case of a break-in or theft attempt. Central locking systems secure all doors simultaneously with a single action. RKE systems allow drivers to remotely lock and unlock their car doors. Each type of security device plays a critical role in enhancing the safety and protection of passenger cars.</p></p>
