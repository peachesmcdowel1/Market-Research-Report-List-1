<p><p>The Insulin Drug Delivery Devices market comprises four main types: syringes, insulin pens, jet injectors, and insulin pumps. Syringes are traditional devices used for insulin injection, requiring manual operation. Insulin pens are user-friendly and enable precise dosing. Jet injectors use a high-pressure stream of insulin to deliver the drug through the skin. Insulin pumps are small devices that continuously deliver insulin through a catheter, providing greater flexibility and control for individuals with diabetes. The market for these devices is driven by their efficacy, ease of use, and the increasing prevalence of diabetes worldwide.</p></p>
