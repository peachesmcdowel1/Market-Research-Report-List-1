<p><p>Sedan and hatchback wheel markets typically include three main types: casting, forging, and other. Forged wheels are made by shaping metal under high pressure, resulting in a stronger and lighter product. The "other" market may include alternative methods of manufacturing wheels, such as flow forming or rotary forging. Each type offers different qualities in terms of durability, weight, and price, catering to the diverse needs of sedan and hatchback owners.</p></p>
