<p><p>Fibrillated Polypropylene Concrete Fiber Market Types include Monofilament Fiber and Multifilament Fiber. Monofilament Fiber is a single-strand fiber that provides high tensile strength and helps in reducing cracking and improving durability of concrete structures. On the other hand, Multifilament Fiber consists of multiple smaller strands twisted together, offering better impact resistance and reducing plastic shrinkage cracks in concrete. Both types of fibers are widely used in construction industry for enhancing the performance and longevity of concrete structures.</p></p>
