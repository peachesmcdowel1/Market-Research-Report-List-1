<p><strong>The Payroll Outsourcing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Managed Outsourcing</li><li>Co-Managed Outsourcing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Payroll outsourcing can be categorized into two market types: Full-Managed Outsourcing and Co-Managed Outsourcing. In Full-Managed Outsourcing, a third-party provider takes complete responsibility for managing all payroll functions, including payroll processing, tax calculations, and employee benefits. On the other hand, Co-Managed Outsourcing involves sharing payroll responsibilities between the organization and the outsourcing provider. The organization retains certain control over payroll processes while the provider handles specific tasks, such as payroll tax reporting or compliance. Both market types offer organizations the advantages of streamlining operations and reducing costs through external expertise and support.</p></p>
