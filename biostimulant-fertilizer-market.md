<p><p>Biostimulant fertilizers are categorized into two main types: acid-based biostimulant fertilizer and plant extract biostimulant fertilizer. Acid-based biostimulant fertilizers are derived from organic acids and are commonly used to improve soil fertility and enhance plant growth. On the other hand, plant extract biostimulant fertilizers are made from natural plant extracts and are known for their ability to stimulate plant growth and development through the activation of various physiological processes. Both types of biostimulant fertilizers play a crucial role in enhancing crop yield and overall plant health.</p></p>
