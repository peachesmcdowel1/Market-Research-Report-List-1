<p><p>Cash counters come in two main market types: Single Pocket and Double Pocket. Single Pocket cash counters have only one pocket for counting and sorting bills, making them suitable for businesses with lower cash volumes. Double Pocket cash counters have two pockets for separating denominations and detecting counterfeit currency, making them ideal for businesses with higher cash flow. Each type offers unique features to cater to different needs, providing efficiency and accuracy in handling cash transactions.</p></p>
