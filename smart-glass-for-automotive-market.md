<p><p>Smart Glass for Automotive refers to glass technology that can change properties such as transparency, color, and light transmission in response to external stimuli. The three main types of smart glass used in automotive applications are Electrochromic, SPD (Suspended Particle Device), and PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal). Electrochromic smart glass is able to change its tint when an electric voltage is applied, while SPD and PDLC smart glass rely on the alignment of particles or liquid crystals to control light transmission.</p></p>
