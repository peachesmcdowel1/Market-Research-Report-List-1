<p><p>X-ray Diffraction Equipment Market consists of two main types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Single-crystal XRD, on the other hand, is used for studying single crystals to understand their atomic and molecular structure in detail. Both types of equipment are essential tools in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, materials science, and geology, for researching and quality control purposes.</p></p>
