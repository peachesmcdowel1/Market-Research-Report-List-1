<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Exhaust System</li><li>Dual Exhaust System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An automotive exhaust system can be classified into two main types: single exhaust system and dual exhaust system. Single exhaust systems have one exhaust pipe that collects and releases exhaust gases from the engine. On the other hand, dual exhaust systems consist of two separate pipes that allow for the simultaneous release of exhaust gases. Dual exhaust systems are often found in high-performance vehicles as they can improve engine performance and provide a more aggressive exhaust note. Both types of exhaust systems play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions and improving overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
