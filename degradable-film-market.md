<p><strong>The Degradable Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edible Film</li><li>Water-Soluble Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Degradable films are types of packaging materials that can break down and decompose naturally over time, reducing their impact on the environment. Edible films are a specific type of degradable film that are made from food-grade materials and can be consumed along with the product it is packaging. Water-soluble films, on the other hand, are degradable films that dissolve in water, making them suitable for applications where packaging needs to be easily removed, such as dishwasher detergents or single-use toiletries. Both edible and water-soluble films offer eco-friendly alternatives to traditional packaging materials.</p></p>
