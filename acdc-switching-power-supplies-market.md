<p><p>AC/DC switching power supplies can be categorized into two main types: Single Output and Multi Output. </p><p>Single Output power supplies provide power to a single load or device. They have a single voltage output, which is suitable for applications that require a single power source.</p><p>On the other hand, Multi Output power supplies provide power to multiple loads or devices simultaneously. They have multiple voltage outputs, allowing them to meet the power needs of different devices or components in one system.</p><p>Both types of power supplies are widely used in various industries, including electronics, telecommunications, and manufacturing, to convert AC voltage to regulated DC voltage for efficient power distribution.</p></p>
