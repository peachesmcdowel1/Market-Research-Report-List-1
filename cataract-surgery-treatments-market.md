<p><p>Cataract surgery treatments market consists of two main types: traditional cataract surgery and laser-assisted cataract surgery. Traditional cataract surgery involves making a small incision in the eye and removing the cloudy lens manually with specialized tools, followed by the implantation of an intraocular lens. Laser-assisted cataract surgery, on the other hand, utilizes laser technology to make precise incisions and soften the cataract for easier removal. This type of surgery is more precise and may offer faster recovery times compared to traditional surgery.</p></p>
