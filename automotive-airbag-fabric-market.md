<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW (One-Piece Woven) Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive airbag fabric comes in two main types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (One-Piece Woven) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is a traditional type that is flat-woven and used in older airbag systems. OPW airbag fabric is a more advanced type that is woven in one continuous piece, providing a higher level of strength and durability. OPW airbag fabric is becoming more popular in modern vehicles due to its improved safety features and reliability. Both types of airbag fabric play a crucial role in protecting occupants during a collision.</p></p>
