<p><p>The military aircraft floor-standing weighing system market is divided into two types: digital and analog. </p><p>Digital weighing systems utilize electronic signals to measure and display the weight of military aircraft. They provide precise and accurate readings, allowing for easy interpretation and recording of data. Digital systems are often equipped with advanced features such as wireless connectivity and data analysis capabilities.</p><p>On the other hand, analog weighing systems use mechanical components to measure weight. They rely on physical mechanisms, such as springs or levers, to determine the aircraft's weight. Although analog systems may lack some of the sophistication and capabilities of digital systems, they are still widely used due to their reliability and simplicity.</p></p>
