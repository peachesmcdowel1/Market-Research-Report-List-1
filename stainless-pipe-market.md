<p><p>Stainless steel pipes are classified into two main types: welded and seamless. Welded pipes are made by welding together pieces of steel to form a continuous pipe, while seamless pipes are created from a single piece of steel without any joints. Welded pipes are commonly used in applications where strength is a priority, while seamless pipes are preferred for high-pressure and high-temperature applications due to their enhanced durability and resistance to corrosion. Both types cater to different market demands based on specific requirements.</p></p>
