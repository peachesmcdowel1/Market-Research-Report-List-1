<p><ul><li>Amines</li><li>Hindered Phenols</li><li>Phosphites</li><li>Thioesters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic antioxidants market types include Amines, Hindered Phenols, Phosphites, Thioesters, and Others. Amines, such as BHA and BHT, interrupt free radical chain reactions. Hindered Phenols, like TBHQ, prevent oxidation by donating hydrogen atoms. Phosphites, such as antioxidants 168 and 215, scavenge reactive oxygen species. Thioesters, like DLTDP, inhibit oxidation by reacting with free radicals. Other synthetic antioxidants, such as ascorbic acid and tocopherols, provide additional protection against oxidation in various applications.</p></p>
