<p><p>Sterilizer steam generators come in two main types: Integral Boiler and Remote Boiler. Integral Boiler generators have the boiler built into the unit, resulting in a more compact and self-contained system. On the other hand, Remote Boiler generators have a separate boiler unit that is located outside of the sterilizer. Both types provide steam for sterilization processes, but the choice between them depends on factors such as space availability, installation preferences, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
