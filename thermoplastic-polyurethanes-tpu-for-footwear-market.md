<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) for footwear market includes three types: Polyester-based TPU, Polyether-based TPU, and Polycaprolactone-based TPU. Polyester-based TPU offers high chemical resistance and excellent flexibility, making it ideal for athletic shoes. Polyether-based TPU provides superior abrasion resistance and resilience, suitable for work boots. Polycaprolactone-based TPU offers exceptional low-temperature flexibility and good hydrolysis resistance, making it suitable for outdoor footwear. Each type of TPU caters to specific footwear needs, providing varying levels of durability, flexibility, and comfort.</p></p>
