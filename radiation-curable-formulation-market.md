<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Curing</li><li>Electron beam (EB) Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation curable formulations refer to materials that can be cured or dried quickly using ultraviolet (UV) or electron beam (EB) radiation. UV curing involves the use of UV light to initiate a chemical reaction that hardens the material, while EB curing utilizes high-energy electrons to achieve the same result. Both technologies offer fast curing times, improved efficiency, and environmental benefits compared to traditional curing methods. The UV curing market is larger and more established, while EB curing is gaining popularity in certain industries due to its unique benefits.</p></p>
