<p><p>Concrete mold release agents are available in two main types - oil based and water based. Oil based mold release agents form a barrier between the mold and the concrete, preventing sticking and enabling easy release of the concrete. Water based mold release agents are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, making them a preferred choice for many users. Both types offer effective release of concrete molds, with oil based agents providing a more durable and long-lasting solution, while water based agents offer a more eco-friendly option.</p></p>
