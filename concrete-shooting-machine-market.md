<p><ul><li>Wet Shooting</li><li>Dry Shooting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete shooting machines are classified into two main types - wet shooting and dry shooting. Wet shooting involves the use of a mixture of concrete and water that is sprayed onto surfaces to create a smooth and even finish. This method is commonly used for applications where a high degree of accuracy and precision is required. On the other hand, dry shooting involves the use of dry concrete mixtures that are sprayed onto surfaces and then activated with water. This method is often used for faster and more efficient applications.</p></p>
