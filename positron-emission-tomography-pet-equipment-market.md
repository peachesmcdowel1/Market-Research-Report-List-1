<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) equipment market can be categorized into three types. PET-CT combines PET and Computed Tomography (CT) technologies to provide detailed images showing both functional and anatomical information. PET-MRI, on the other hand, combines PET with Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) to provide images that depict both metabolism and anatomy. Other types of PET equipment may include standalone PET scanners or devices focusing on specific applications. These different types of PET equipment allow for various imaging capabilities to assist in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases.</p></p>
