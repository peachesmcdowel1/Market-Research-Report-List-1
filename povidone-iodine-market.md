Additionally, the growing prevalence of chronic wounds and injuries, such as pressure ulcers and diabetic foot ulcers, is contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising geriatric population, who are more susceptible to infections, is also fueling the demand for povidone-iodine products.
